Symptoms of Covid-19

{# #} {# #}

The symptoms of New Coronavirus are not very serious in the early stage. Fever, fatigue and dry cough are the main manifestations. A small number of patients with nasal congestion, runny nose, sore throat and diarrhea and other symptoms.
